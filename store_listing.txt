SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars):
Get a loud alarm the moment train seats open up on your route.

FULL DESCRIPTION:
Never miss a confirmed train seat again!

Train Seat Alert watches Indian Railways seat availability for you and sounds a
loud alarm the instant seats become available on your train — even when your phone
is locked.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Add your train: train number, boarding & destination stations, journey date,
   travel class (SL, 3A, 2A, etc.) and quota.
2. Set a seat threshold and how often to check (every 15 min to 12 hours).
3. Relax. The app checks automatically in the background.
4. When seats open up, you get a full-screen alarm with ringtone and vibration.

FEATURES
• Track unlimited trains at once
• Automatic background checking (battery-friendly)
• Loud full-screen alarm that works on the lock screen
• Snooze and dismiss controls
• Availability history with a seat-count chart
• Pause / resume any alert
• Home-screen widget showing your most urgent alert
• Dark mode and Material You theming
• Works automatically after a phone restart

Perfect for booking Tatkal, waitlist clearing, or grabbing last-minute seats.

Note: Seat data is provided via a third-party railway information API and is for
reference only. Always confirm on the official IRCTC platform before booking.
