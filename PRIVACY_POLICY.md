# Privacy Policy — Train Seat Alert

_Last updated: 11 June 2026_

Train Seat Alert ("the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy
explains what information the app handles and how.

## Information the app stores
All data you enter (train numbers, station codes, journey dates, travel class,
thresholds, and check history) is stored **only on your device** in a local
database. None of this information is transmitted to or stored on any server
operated by us.

## Information sent to third parties
To check live seat availability, the app sends the train number, station codes,
journey date, travel class, and quota to the **RapidAPI IRCTC service**
(`irctc1.p.rapidapi.com`) over an encrypted HTTPS connection. This is required to
retrieve seat availability. Please review RapidAPI's privacy policy at
https://rapidapi.com/privacy/ for how they handle requests.

## Information we do NOT collect
- We do not collect personal identifiers (name, email, phone, location).
- We have no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- We do not create user accounts.

## Permissions
- **Internet** — to query seat availability.
- **Notifications / Full-screen intent** — to alert you when seats become available.
- **Vibrate / Wake lock** — for the alarm.
- **Receive boot completed** — to resume your alerts after the phone restarts.

## Data deletion
Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data. You may also
delete individual alerts from within the app at any time.

## Children's privacy
The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data
from them.
